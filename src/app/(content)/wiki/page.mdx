# Ricing Guide

Welcome to the easyrice ricing guide. Whether you're new to Linux customization or a seasoned ricer, this wiki covers everything you need to create a beautiful desktop.

## What You'll Learn

- **What ricing is** and why people do it
- How to choose and configure a **window manager**
- Managing your **dotfiles** for reproducible setups
- Tips and tricks for making your desktop truly yours

## Where to Start

If you're completely new, start with [What is Ricing?](/wiki/what-is-ricing) for an introduction to the concept and culture.

Ready to dive in? Check out [Window Managers](/wiki/window-managers) to pick the foundation of your rice.
